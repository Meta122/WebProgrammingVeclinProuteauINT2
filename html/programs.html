Computer Science Department

Discover our programs and dive into the world of digital innovation

The IT team's program is designed to train agile technical experts capable of adapting to the rapid evolutions of the digital sector. It is built primarily on acquiring a very solid technical foundation. Students quickly dive into the core of software development. The focus is on advanced algorithmics and the perfect mastery of essential programming languages such as Python, Java, or C++.

Beyond pure coding, the learning process encompasses the entire digital infrastructure. The coursework covers network architecture, operating systems administration, and database design in detail. The curriculum then allows students to move towards cutting-edge technologies. They specialize in highly strategic fields such as cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, data science, or Cloud architecture.

Finally, theory gives way to a major focus on professional practice. Skills are acquired through numerous group projects, consistently combining technical execution with project management and teamwork.
